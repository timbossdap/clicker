🎮 GAME FEATURES:

  Click Button:
    • 280×280px big button in center
    • Click to earn score
    • Multiplied by all active multipliers

  Left Panel (Upgrades):
    • 4 multiplier tiers at left edge
    • Reset button (wipes score & multipliers)
    • Rebirth button (resets + 2x multiplier)
    • Gacha Shop button (spend tokens)

  Right Panel (When Shop Open):
    • Common Pack (1 token) → +0.1x
    • Rare Pack (2 tokens) → +0.3x
    • Epic Pack (3 tokens) → +0.8x
    • Legendary Pack (5 tokens) → +2.0x
    • Close button to hide shop

  Top Display:
    • Score (formatted: 1.5K, 2.3M, 1.2B, 1.5T)
    • Active multipliers (only > 1.0x shown)
    • Rebirth status
